roguelight manual

roguelight

controls:

use wasd or the arrow keys to move the cursor
use space to select units
use space to confirm a move
press e or esc to end your turn


units:

leader - no stat bonuses. you lose if your leader is captured.
warrior - +1 def and +1 atk.
rogue - -1 def. gets two moves per turn.
mage - +2 atk.
ranger - capturing units doesn't use up a turn. stays in place when capturing an adjacent unit.


combat:

units may capture any other unit with defense lower than or equal to it's attack.


special tiles:

village - when a unit moves onto this tile, it recruits an additional random unit and places it
in an empty adjacent tile.
stairs - when a unit moves onto this tile, you progress to the next level.
